How to Play

Game Rules

  • Each player has 3 pieces — You play X, AI plays O
  • Phase 1 — Placing: Click empty cells to place your pieces
  • Phase 2 — Moving: After all 3 pieces are placed, click a piece to grab it, then click an empty cell to move it
  • First to get 3 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins!

Good luck. Can you beat the AI?
